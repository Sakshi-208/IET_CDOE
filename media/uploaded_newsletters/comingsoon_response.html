 Centre of Distance and Online Education offers programmes of the study that are customized to meet the learning requirements of knowledge seekers as well as to ensure that they learn at their own pace and convenience.

  • Comfort of learning from your own home.

  • You can study at your own pace.

Benefits of Distance Learning

  • It’s less expensive than traditional education.
  • Location is not a problem.
  • Learning is flexible and self-paced.
  • Possible networking with like minded students.
  • Learning is more efficient
